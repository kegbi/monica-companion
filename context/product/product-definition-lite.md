# Monica Companion — Product Summary

## Vision

Enable MonicaHQ users to capture and retrieve relationship information effortlessly through voice and text messages in Telegram. Talk into your phone after a call, and AI handles the rest — transcribing, identifying the contact, and saving to MonicaHQ.

## Target Audience

Individuals using MonicaHQ as a personal CRM — from relationship-focused people who want to remember birthdays and life events, to busy professionals who need to log networking details on the go.

## Core Features

- **Voice & Text Processing** — Send voice or text messages via Telegram; AI transcribes and parses intent.
- **Smart Contact Resolution** — AI identifies contacts, disambiguates when needed, skips confirmation when unambiguous.
- **Basic CRUD** — Create contacts, add notes, log activities, update details, query information.
- **Configurable Confirmation** — Auto-execute or require approval; always clarifies ambiguity.
- **Daily/Weekly Reminders** — Cron job for event summaries (birthdays, reminders) at a chosen time.
- **Multi-User Support** — Isolated accounts with independent MonicaHQ connections.
- **Modular Architecture** — Pluggable connector layer for future messaging platforms.
